Art Heals Raising Funds via Art for Hope

  • We are one of the contributing artists for the event of Art for Hope. Art for Hope is an online art auction to feature many talented and generous artists from our community and beyond, supporting children receiving critical hospitality services- like housing, meals and community- to their families. The Art Auction takes place from May 15-May 19, 2023. If you are interested in donating a piece together with us, we would be more than grateful for your support! You can contact us at arthealsfw@gmail.com. Thank you!

Art Heals Raised Funds for Hope's Harbor!

  • On April 20, 2023, Art Heals was on the top list of Hope’s Harbor fundraisers and raised hundreds of dollars in the event Give Greater Allen County. We believe our efforts make a difference for the nonprofits we love and make our community a better place to live, work and play. We are proud of our growing philanthropy and all that is good in our community. Way to go!

22 April 2023

Art Heals Fundraising on Give Greater Allen County Day

  • On April 20, Art Heals will be participating in Give Greater Allen County day. This is a one-day, 12-hour initiative that fosters individual philanthropy in Allen County and is hosted by the Community Foundation of Greater Fort Wayne. Last year Hope’s Harbor participated in this virtual day of giving and received some very generous donations from the community. This year Art Heals has a unique fundraising page on behalf of Hope’s Harbor on this day. We are so grateful for your continued support of our organization and the families we serve. Here is a link to our fundraising page that you can share with your friends and family members. https://www.givegreaterallen.com/p2p/297045/sophia-wang-3e0b04d6-c4ca-416c-92c3-f050f777bb23 The event runs on April 20 only, from 7am-7pm. Thank you for your support!

Sophia Zhang Representing Art Heals Donated Artworks to Art for Hope

  • We are proudly announcing that our Co-founder Sophia Zhang is one of the contributing artists in the Art For Hope. On May 23-27, 2022, Sophia made donations with two of her paintings to raise money for the Mad Anthony Children’s Hope House, which a non-profit organization that provides rooms for families whose children have to stay hospitalized for extended periods of time.
