Our Purpose

At ArtHeals, we believe in the power of art to heal and bring joy to people's lives. That's why we have created a unique charity project that enables you to give back in a truly meaningful way. By donating your artwork to us, you have the opportunity to make a difference in the lives of those in need. Our team will carefully curate and sell your artwork, with all proceeds going directly to support hospitals and the critical work they do in providing medical care and comfort to those who need it most. Specifically, we will be donating the proceeds to Parkview Women's and Children's Hospital and Hope's Harbor. Join us in using art to heal, inspire, and make a positive impact on the world.
